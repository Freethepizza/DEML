This is DEML,The Don't Expect a Markup Language way of making the web.

DEML is an HTML preprocessor with a super easy syntax even the dumbest person you know would be able to make nice looking websites in minutes!

    ROADMAP(Official):

  • Simple syntax for lists.
  • Styling
  • Attributes for tags.
  • Parsing improvements.
  • WebApp
  • Universal styling (CSS integrated mini framework).


    ROADMAP(Unofficial):

  • Text editor.
  • Blogging platform.
  • SASS/LESS integration.
  • C/C++ Version.
